Cadence & ownership

The cadence that turns a roadmap into a working partnership.

01 — Cadence
Weekly working session
Same time each week. Working session, not status update. Open decisions, prioritisation calls, demos of what shipped, blockers to close. Async update on progress in between.
02 — Ownership
Named DRI on both sides
One person accountable per side, end-to-end. Escalation path defined in week one so we don't lose days to unclear ownership when something needs a call.
03 — Measurement
One renewal-defining metric
Everything ladders to a single number we both agree on. Aligned in week one. Other metrics are diagnostics.
